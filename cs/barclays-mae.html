Mortgage Application Experience (MAE)

Simplifying every step of the mortgage journey for brokers, advisers and customers.

The Mortgage Application Experience (MAE) is a multi-year rebuild of Barclays’ digital mortgage platform – transforming how brokers and advisers manage applications from first quote to final offer.

As Content Design Lead, I shaped how the language, logic and structure work together – creating reusable patterns for complex financial rules, simplifying eligibility and income checks, and designing error and help content that guides users with clarity and confidence. Working closely with designers and developers, I used content design to make automation and policy logic feel effortless for the user.

The results were clear. Application times dropped from nearly an hour to under 15 minutes. Referrals to underwriters fell sharply thanks to clearer guidance and automated checks. The platform, now used by all 26,000 brokers, has seen satisfaction move from deeply negative to strongly positive – the highest levels ever recorded in broker surveys – and has helped drive record lending volumes.
